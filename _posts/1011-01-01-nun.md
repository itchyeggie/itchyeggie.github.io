Animated Short directed by Heta Jäälinoja  
Visual Researcher

During Covid time, Heta asked me what would be my dream task to work for a film. I answered "Exploring the visuals!" - She create this dream task and hire me for visual researching. I was given the mood board, inspirational drawings from Karin Paumer, some stills from Heta and freehand to explore its potential. It was still one of my favourtie work. The Final Design was completed by Heta & Sanni Lahtinen.
